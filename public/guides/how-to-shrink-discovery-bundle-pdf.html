How to Shrink Discovery Bundle PDF for eDiscovery

You have compiled thousands of pages of scanned documents, emails, and evidence into a court bundle. The file is 500MB. The court's e-filing system crashes every time you try to upload it.

eDiscovery involves sharing massive amounts of documentation. Scanned documents ("images" of text) are particularly heavy. While modern PDF software attempts to optimize them, Paralegals often need to adhere to strict portal limits (often under 50MB per file) without splitting the bundle into 10 confusing parts.

Why File Size is a Problem for Law Firms
