How to Compress Textbook PDF for iPad Reading

You bought the digital version of your 1,200-page biology textbook. You open it in GoodNotes or Notability on your iPad, and scrolling is a nightmare. The pages render slowly, and highlighting lags. The file is simply too dense.

Why File Size is a Problem for Students
