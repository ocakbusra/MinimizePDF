How to Compress Product Catalog PDF for Web Sales

A potential B2B customer visits your site and clicks "Download Wholesale Catalog". If it takes 2 minutes to download because the file is 80MB, they might just leave. Speed is conversion.

Product catalogs are dense. They contain hundreds of product photos, SKU numbers, and layout elements. While high-res is great for print catalog distribution, your web version should be lean. A file size under 10MB is the target for instant gratification.

Why File Size is a Problem for Sales

Large files hurt your website's performance and SEO. They cost you more in bandwidth fees. But most importantly, they create friction for the buyer, especially if they are browsing your site on a mobile device with spotty reception.
