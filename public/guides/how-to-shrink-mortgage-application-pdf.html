How to Shrink Mortgage Application PDF for Bank Portals

You have a mortgage application ready to submit to the lender. It's close to the deadline. You try to upload the supporting documents—payslips, bank statements, tax returns, ID scans—and the bank's portal crashes or rejects the file. "File too large."

In the mortgage industry, speed is critical. A delayed application can mean a client loses their dream home or a rate lock expires. You need a reliable way to get bulky scanned documents into the system without degrading their quality so underwriters can still read them easily.

Why File Size is a Problem for Brokers

Mortgage applications are paper-heavy. Even digital applications involve scanning physical documents. When you bundle 3 months of bank statements, 2 years of tax returns, and color ID scans into one PDF, the size explodes. Bank portals often have legacy limits (sometimes 20MB or less) that are totally out of sync with modern scanner resolutions.
