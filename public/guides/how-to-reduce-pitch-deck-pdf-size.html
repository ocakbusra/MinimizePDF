Why File Size is a Problem for Consultants

Great pitch decks are visually rich. They contain:

  • High-definition product mockups.
  • Complex vector graphs and charts.
  • Full-page background images.
  • Custom corporate typography.

While these elements sell your story, they bloat the file. A 15-slide deck can easily reach 50MB. Most corporate email servers reject incoming attachments larger than 20MB, and Outlook often struggles with anything over 10MB.
