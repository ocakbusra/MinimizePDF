How to Compress Travel Itinerary PDF for Mobile Access

Your client just landed in Tokyo. They try to open their travel itinerary PDF to find the hotel address, but the 20MB file is taking forever to download on expensive roaming data. A frustrated client is not a repeat client.

Modern travel itineraries are visual masterpieces—full of hotel photos, maps, and rich graphics. While they look great, they are often too heavy for practical travel use. Travelers need documents that load instantly on their phones, even with 1 bar of 3G signal.

Why File Size is a Problem for Travelers

Data roaming is expensive and unreliable. Large PDF files can fail to download entirely in areas with poor connectivity (like airports or remote resorts). Compressing the itinerary ensures that the essential information—booking numbers, addresses, and times—is always accessible.
