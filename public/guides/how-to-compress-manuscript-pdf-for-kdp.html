How to Compress Manuscript PDF for KDP & IngramSpark

Your book is finished. The cover is perfect. But when you upload the interior file to Kindle Direct Publishing (KDP) or IngramSpark, it fails. Use "High Quality" mode to reduce size without sacrificing print sharpness.

Self-publishing platforms have strict file size limits (often around 650MB for KDP, but practically much lower for smooth processing). If you have written a photo-heavy cookbook, a graphic novel, or an academic text with charts, your PDF can easily balloon to 1GB. Compressing it improperly results in blurry images that look amateurish in print.
