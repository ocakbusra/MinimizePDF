How to Optimize Photo Contact Sheets for Client Email

You just finished a shoot. You export a contact sheet PDF from Lightroom so the client can pick their favorites. But the PDF is 80MB. You can't email it. You have to use WeTransfer, adding an extra step that slows down their reply.

Proofing should be frictionless. You want your client to open the PDF directly in their email app on their phone and start picking shots. Massive files create friction. They take time to download and eat up data plans.

Why File Size is a Problem for Photographers

Lightroom and Capture One produce high-quality PDFs by default. Even "Draft" quality can be surprisingly large if you have hundreds of photos in the set. You need a way to make the file tiny without making the photos look pixelated or unappealing.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Resize Proofs
